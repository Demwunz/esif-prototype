Financial (3 of 5) : Cost and Funding Allocations

Cost and funding allocations are used to indicate the split costs and funding between Priority Axes, Investment Priorities, LEP areas and Categroies of region. It is used to determine the different grant rates that may apply to different categories of region and/or different investment priorities. The split of match funding sources between public sources (e.g local authority funding) and private sources (e.g private sector reserves, bank loan, SME contributions) is to meet our reporting obligations to the European Commission where have to report not just on the {Fund eg ERDF} funding but also on the public and private funding related to the expenditure.
