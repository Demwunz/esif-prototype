Privacy policy

Any information in your application will be treated confidentially and will only be released if legally required, eg due to a Freedom of Information request.

If you think any sensitive information in your application shouldn't be disclosed then you must contact Department for Communities and Local Government (DCLG) or Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) explaining what information you think is sensitive and why.

Use of your personal information

DCLG and DWP may:

  • publish information about your application on their websites
  • share information with relevant agencies for the purposes of fraud prevention
  • share anonymised data with third parties to allow statistical analysis and reporting

Your information may also be used in internal and external reviews of departmental policies and procedures.

Data retention

Your information will be held for 3 years after the programme has closed (currently expected to be 2032).

Your information could be held for longer if there's a legitimate reason to do so, for example:

  • there are legal proceedings concerning your application
  • it's required by law or a regulatory body

If you think that any personal information held about you is wrong then contact DCLG or DWP to have it corrected.
